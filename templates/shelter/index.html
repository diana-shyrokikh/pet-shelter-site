About Us

We are a dedicated and compassionate animal rescue organization committed to providing a safe haven for animals in need. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray, abandoned, and surrendered animals, ultimately giving them a second chance at a loving and forever home.

We believe that every animal deserves a chance to be cherished and cared for. With a team of experienced staff members and a network of passionate volunteers, we work tirelessly to ensure the physical and emotional well-being of the animals under our care.

Animal Care

Our shelter provides a temporary sanctuary where animals receive the love, nourishment, and medical attention they require. We offer a nurturing environment that promotes socialization and helps animals regain their trust in humans.

Whether it's a playful kitten, a senior dog seeking companionship, or a misunderstood breed looking for a fresh start, we welcome all animals with open arms. We have a dedicated team of staff members, volunteers, including veterinaries, who provides veterinary care, even after adopting pet.

Adoption Service

Through our diligent adoption process, we strive to find the perfect match between our animals and loving families. We carefully screen potential adopters to ensure responsible ownership and a compatible lifestyle, taking into consideration the unique needs and personalities of each animal.

The process of adoption is the next:

  1. You can pick any pet you like on our website and then connect with us to arrange the meting with the pet.
  2. In case you and pet like each other, and you are ready to take our pet in the family, we start adoption process.
  3. After successful screening, we create your account on our website to keep in touch with you and to open our support program for you.

Our support program

After adoption our pet you will become a participant automatically in our support program.

The support program includes:

  • a free consultation with our veterinary specialists, whose contacts you will find in your profile;
  • the discounts on some animal feed in our partner zoo shop by promo-code on your profile;
  • the discounts on vaccinations in our partner vet clinic by promo-code on your profile;
  • our helps in any question connected with pet.
